Our Story

Minaar is founded by 2 friends who met 2 decades ago while studying hotel management in Hyderabad, bonded via their love for food and travel. ​After completing their course, they went their separate ways, one a Celebrated Chef at a top 5-star hotel and the other, a top management executive in the banking industry. Only to be brought back together by their passion for food and a zeal to create innovative culinary experiences. Minaar is an ode to friendship, food and flavours and friendship.
